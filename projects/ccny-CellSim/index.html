About Our Game

Making a game that the player will really enjoy is the goal of its developper. CellSim is a game that we expect to fully satisfy it player. CellSim is a simulation of a fight between good cells and bad cell inside the human body. Ofcourse, the player will be on the good cells side. The good cell(s) is called the defender. when the defender touches any other cell, there is a positive or negative effect that occurs based on the type of cell touched. That will be explained in the table below. His role will be to quicky identify and destroy the bad cells (bacteria or viruses) invaders.
Focus, move fast, distroy as many enemies as possible. Be the winner and feel the accomplishment.
CellSim was Developed by Gabriel Espinoza, and Ide Tafaba Tchapouh.
