About Our Game

Axis Break was inspired by the classic breakout game.
Complete all levels by taking control of the paddle to deflect the ball and break all bricks.
Axis Break was Developed by Luigi Vingo, Gabriel Espinoza, and Ide Tafaba Tchapouh.

Take Full Control of The Paddle

Left Arrow = Move Left
Right Arrow = Move Right
Up Arrow = Rotate Right
Down Arrow = Rotate Left

Rotating Bricks

Rotating bricks affect the direction of the ball making it more unpredictable, which makes Axis Break more fun and challenging.

Types of Bricks

There are 3 types of bricks. Green bricks are the easies to break and require 2 hits to break. Orange bricks require more than 3 hits to break. Finally, Red bricks are the hardest to break and require more hits than the Orange bricks.
